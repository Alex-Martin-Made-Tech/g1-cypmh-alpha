Based on what you've told us, we think these are the best ways to get help with what you're feeling right now:

Gloucestershire specialist mental health services

You can refer yourself to Child and Adolescent Mental Health (CAMHS) in Gloucestershire. They provide specialist NHS mental health services for children and young people who are experiencing moderate to severe mental health difficulties.

Gloucestershire self-harm helpline

Phone, text and web-chat helpline. A trained mental health recovery worker will listen to you, support you, provide options and ways of coping and give information of other places that can help if needed. 5pm - 10pm every day.

Do you need immediate help?

Urgent advice: Call our Crisis Team on 0800 169 0398 if:

  • you are having suicidal thoughts and feel like you are a risk to yourself
  • you are worried that someone you care for is at risk
