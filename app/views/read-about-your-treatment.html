The CAMHS Process

Once you've submitted a request for support, a member of the Children's Mental Health eco-system will contact you to discuss this further. They will ask you more questions about what kind of help you want and what youâ€™re expecting.

After this, a clinically trained member of staff will have suggested that the best support option for you right now is CAMHS. You will then be placed in a waiting-list for treatment, based on your presenting needs.

Your progress will be reviewed regularly with you, your family/carers your Care Plan will be updated as necessary.

Based on your progress/ achievement of agreed goals, your family/carers will be involved in any discussion and decisions about discharge from CAMHS. CAMHS will work with you and your family/carers to identify support to help you stay healthy and to continue to be well.
