Making pages

Create all your HTML pages in the app/views folder.

All pages need the .html extension.

Pages within app/views will be automatically served. For example, if you create a page called contact.html and then go to localhost:3000/help in your browser, you will see that page.

Adding your pages into folders also works.

For example, you can make the page views/medicines/ibuprofen.html, and then view the page by going to localhost:3000/medicines/ibuprofen.
