Making pages

Create all your HTML pages in the app/views folder.

All pages should have the .html extension.

All pages will be automatically served. For example if you add a template called help.html and then go to localhost:3000/help in your browser, you will see that page.

Folders for pages

Folders also work, so you can make the template views/account/profile.html, and then view the page by going to localhost:3000/account/profile.
